Design Principles
i.
Navy as warmth

Deep navy (#1A2B42) has a warm cast — it reads as sophisticated and approachable, not cold or corporate.

ii.
Playfair for gravitas

Playfair's high-contrast strokes signal quality and longevity — your wardrobe is an investment, not a subscription.

iii.
Cornflower precision

The accent (#3D6494) sits between navy and sky — readable, elegant, and distinct from generic corporate blue.

iv.
Warm ivory base

The background (#FAFAF8) has a warm yellow undertone — it reads as quality paper, not a bright screen.
