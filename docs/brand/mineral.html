Design Principles
🔵
Steel, not sky

The accent (#5B7FA0) sits in the desaturated middle — distinctly blue but never corporate or cold. Closer to weathered denim than a dashboard.

⚡
One font, five weights

DM Sans from 300–700 creates a complete typographic system without any serif. Clean, tech-adjacent, trusted.

📐
Cool system

Unlike Studio's warmth, Mineral leans cool — backgrounds have a subtle blue-grey cast. It's systematic but never sterile.

🌫️
Elevation via shadow

Depth comes from subtle box-shadows (0 1px 3px), not borders. Cards float slightly above the surface.
