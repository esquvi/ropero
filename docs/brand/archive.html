Design Principles
Clothes have stories

Wear logs, notes, and memories are first-class features — not afterthoughts. The interface encourages reflection.

Warmth over precision

Rounded corners (14–20px), warm ambers, and Lora's literary character signal approachability over utility.

Serif as soul

Lora at display sizes gives the brand a human quality — it reads like a well-worn notebook, not a spreadsheet.

Parchment, not white

The background is warm parchment — it evokes paper, memory, and time. Never a clinical white surface.
