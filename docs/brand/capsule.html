Design Principles
I
One accent per view

The blush appears on the primary CTA, active filter, or selected state — never more than once per screen.

II
Garamond for feeling

The serif is reserved for headings and display text only. Body is Inter. This creates a quiet tension.

III
Generous space

Whitespace is the dominant design element. More space signals quality, never emptiness.

IV
Warm whites only

Pure white for cards and inputs. Off-white for backgrounds. Never clinical blue-white tones.
