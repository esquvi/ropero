BASalt

Cobalt palette · Rufina + Raleway typography

The deep blue of Cobalt made warm and approachable. Where Cobalt's Cinzel felt architectural and Roman, Rufina brings old-world book culture — the blue of ink on fine paper. Raleway grounds it in modern clarity. Basalt is a wardrobe guide that feels like a well-annotated catalogue raisonné: intellectual but livable.
