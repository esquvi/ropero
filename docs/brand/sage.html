Brand Principles
01.
Tactile warmth
Cream and sage are the colours of natural fibres — wool, linen, cotton. The UI should feel like your favourite jumper.
02.
Generous radius
At 12px, corners are soft without being childlike. Every surface feels approachable, like something you want to touch.
03.
Fraunces as anchor
Fraunces is a soft-contrast optical serif — expressive at large sizes, elegant at small. It carries emotional warmth.
04.
Ritual over task
Getting dressed is a ritual. Sage makes logging a wear feel like journaling, not data entry. Tone matters everywhere.
