Design Principles
🔥
Warmth with function

Terracotta and warm neutrals make a data-dense interface feel human. The tool should feel like a trusted colleague.

⚡
Density earns its place

Stats, wear counts, and metadata are visible without being overwhelming. Data builds confidence, not anxiety.

🎯
One font, all weights

Plus Jakarta Sans from 300–800 creates hierarchy without mixing typefaces — clean, systematic, reliable.

🌿
Warm system, not cold

Every gray has a brown undertone. Every shadow is warm-tinted. The background is off-white, never pure white.
