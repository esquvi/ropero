Terroir

Matcha palette · Fraunces + DM Sans typography

Matcha's clean green and gold palette warmed considerably by Fraunces' optical personality. Where Matcha felt precise and cool, Terroir feels grown — like a wardrobe assembled with taste over time rather than purchased all at once. The italic weight adds an effortless quality that Josefin Sans never could.
