Design Principles
i.
Dark is the default

The dark experience is designed first. Light mode exists but is a considered alternative, not the primary canvas.

ii.
Gold as signal

Champagne gold on near-black creates a luminous accent. Used for active states, CTAs, and selection — never decoration.

iii.
Warm darks only

Every dark background has a brown undertone (#1A1714). Cold blacks (#000, #111) are explicitly forbidden.

iv.
Sharp edges, always

Maximum 6px border radius. No pill shapes on containers. Precision and restraint communicate luxury better than softness.
