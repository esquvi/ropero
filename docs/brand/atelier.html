Design Principles
01
Restraint over decoration

Gold appears on one element per view. Every ornamental choice must earn its place.

02
Editorial scale

Display type at 64–88px isn't excessive — it signals that your wardrobe deserves this presence.

03
Warm, never cold

Every neutral has a warm undertone. Ivory over white. Charcoal over black. Never clinical.

04
Typography-led

Cormorant at large sizes does more design work than any graphic element could.
